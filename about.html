About Quick PDF Editor

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Quick PDF Editor is a free online tool for editing PDF documents directly in your web browser. There is no account to create, nothing to install, and nothing to pay for.

What you can do

  • Edit text in an existing PDF: click a line and type to change it in place.
  • Add text anywhere on a page, with control over font, size, bold and italic.
  • Sign documents by drawing, typing, or uploading a signature image.
  • Erase existing content, such as an old signature or stray marks.
  • Stamp pages with labels like Approved, Rejected, Final, Draft or Copy.
  • Reorder pages: drag to rearrange, delete, or insert blank pages.

How it works

Most editing happens locally, right inside your browser. For a small number of operations that need high-fidelity rendering, your file may be sent over a secure HTTPS connection to our processing service, where it is handled temporarily in memory to produce your edited PDF. We do not intentionally retain files after processing, and we do not store your documents in a database. You can read the details in our Privacy Policy.

Our principles

  • Free to use: no charges, no trials, no paywalls.
  • No account required: open a PDF and start editing immediately.
  • No subscriptions: nothing to cancel, ever.
  • No ads: a clean, distraction-free workspace.
  • No permanent storage: your files are not kept after you are done.
  • Secure connection: the site is served over HTTPS.

Who it is for

Anyone who needs to make a quick change to a PDF (filling in a form, signing a contract, fixing a typo, or tidying up pages) without uploading sensitive documents to a service that keeps them or asking you to sign up first.

Questions or feedback?

We would love to hear from you. Reach us any time at our contact page or email support@quickpdfeditor.com.
