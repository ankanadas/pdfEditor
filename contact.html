Contact us

We usually reply within a couple of business days.

Have a question, found a bug, or want to share feedback? We would genuinely love to hear from you. Send a message below — or email us directly. Both reach the same inbox.

Or email us directly

support@quickpdfeditor.com

What to include

To help us respond quickly, it is useful if you can tell us:

  • What you were trying to do;
  • What happened instead (and any error message you saw);
  • Your browser and device (for example, "Chrome on Windows" or "Safari on iPhone").
Please do not email us your private PDFs. We don't need your documents to help, and we don't permanently store files. A short description or a screenshot with sensitive details removed is plenty.

What we can help with

  • Questions about how to use the editor;
  • Bug reports and unexpected behaviour;
  • Feature requests and feedback;
  • Privacy questions or requests (see our Privacy Policy);
  • Security reports (see our security.txt).
